Enjoy nature at its finest! Squire Creek offers RV and tent camping among the beautiful moss-laden trees of an old growth forest. The park is a perfect staging area for adventures into the Mt Baker - Snoqualmie National Forest, hiking to the base of Whitehorse Mountain, or participation in the Darrington Bluegrass Festival. Two large picnic shelters are located near the crystal clear waters of the creek which bears the park&#39;s name.

drivingDirection:

From I-5 take State Route 530 east through Arlington; drive 26 miles toward Darrington, look for signs, park entrance is on the left.
