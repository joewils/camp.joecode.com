This 39 acre lakeside park is nestled in a beautiful forest setting. Originally constructed in the 1970&#39;s, this park has much to offer and is close to historic downtown Snohomish and just a few miles from US Hwy 2, gateway to the Cascade Mountain Range and Stevens Pass. The park offers a boat launch, camping, cabins, swimming (summer), picnicking, fishing, a playground, and biking and walking on park roads. There are developed campsites with water and electric hookup (30 amp), standard sites with no hookup and ADA accessible sites with hookup (30 amp). Each site comes with a picnic table and fire pit with grill.
