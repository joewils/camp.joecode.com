Glade Creek Campground is a group site. The campground is located along US Highway 12 on the Clearwater National Forest about 7 miles east of Lowell, Idaho. Located in the mountains of north central Idaho, the forested site offers campers river access and beautiful scenery. The site is suitable for groups up to 35 people and is perfect for families and groups who want easy access to the Lochsa River. Fees are $10/night Sunday to Thursday, $25/night weekends and holidays.

drivingDirection:

Glade Creek Campground is located at about milepost 105 on US Highway 12. It is approximately 31 miles east of Kooskia and about seven miles east of Lowell, Idaho.

facilitiesDescription:

Glade Creek has four campsites and encompasses about one acre. Two of the sites are located adjacent to the river. There is a group fire ring with benches also adjacent to the river. All four sites have accessible fire rings, ten foot picnic tables, level tent pads and graveled parking areas. Site 2 has an extra table and several tent sites. The spurs are short and not suitable for RVs greater than 24 feet. The campground has one vault toilet. There is no water system or dumpster at this site. Campers may use the water and dumpster at Apgar campground, one mile west on Highway 12. There are no power, water or sewer hook-ups at any of the sites.

There is no dumpster, garbage cans or garbage service at this site. Pack it in, Pack it out. There is a dumpster at Apgar campground, one mile west on Highway 12. Gate will be unlocked and guest may keep it open or closed at their discretion. A maximum capacity of 15 passenger vehicles is allowed. This site can accomodate up to 4 travel trailers less than 24 feet long. Each travel trailer will reduce available parking. Plan Accordingly. All vehicles and trailers must remain on hardened surfaces within the campsite parking spur. Guest must perform general site clean-up and trash removal prior to departure. Maximum length of the reservation is 13 nights/14 days.
