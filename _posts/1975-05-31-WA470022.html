Located on the eastern shore of Lake Goodwin, this 45 acre park offers water access, camping, two picnic shelters, playground, 465 feet of swim beach, 1,140 feet of freshwater shoreline, a concession stand, a two-lane concrete boat launch and walking trails. Management of the park was transferred to Snohomish County from the Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission on July 16, 2009.
