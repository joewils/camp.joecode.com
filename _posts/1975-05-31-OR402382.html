Situated in one of the most scenic areas on the Oregon coast, Sunset Bay State Park features beautiful sandy beaches protected by towering sea cliffs. The park has 29 full hookup, 34 electrical, 66 individual tent sites and two group tent areas, as well as 8 yurts. MAKE SURE YOUR SITE MEETS YOUR CAMPING NEEDS

Before reserving, please become familiar with the particulars of your chosen site, including the size of the tent pad area and the type of equipment that you may bring to the park. Site details and tent pad sizes can be viewed online by clicking on the site name.
