Campground, sheltered by trees, located at 100 ft. elevation. 1/4 mile from the beach. Campsites for RV&#39;s, tents, and even yurts. Accessible flush facilities &amp; hot showers provided in campground. Semi-private sites with vegetative visual barriers. Trails to beach from campground. Cable connection available at selected sites. Picnic area located on beach with flush facilities &amp; potable water. Playground available in campground. Campground within city limits, all services in walking distance. Close to golf course, redwoods, wilderness, marina, trails and rivers.
