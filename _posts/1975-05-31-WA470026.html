McCollum Pioneer Park is a 78 acre family-oriented park, with a rich combination of natural habitats that supports a wide variety of both active and passive recreation opportunities. Its presence serves as a living memorial to the original pioneers who settled this area. Our heated outdoor (junior Olympic size) pool offers swim lessons during the summer months and open swim each afternoon. Take a morning stroll on our nature trails or bring your family and friends out for a picnic. Conveniently located 1/4 mile from I-5.

drivingDirection:

From I-5 southbound take exit 186, turn left onto 128th St SW / WA-96 for 1/4 mile, park entrance is on your right. From I-5 northbound take exit 186, turn right onto 128th St SW / WA-96 for 1/4 mile, park entrance is on your right.
