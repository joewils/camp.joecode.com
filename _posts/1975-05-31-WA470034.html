WELCOME TO EVERGREEN STATE FAIRGROUNDS RV2

The following information has been established to insure a pleasant stay for all users while involved in events on the Evergreen State Fairgrounds.

RV park users and their visitors shall comply with Snohomish County rules and regulations. If at any time any or all members of the group fail to comply, they must immediately vacate fairgrounds when asked to leave.

1.

RV PERMITS must be placed inside RV or trailer in a window location highly visible to outside RV lot attendants.

2.

IMPOUNDMENT OR EVICTION. Any vehicle not displaying appropriate RV parking pass my be subject to eviction from the grounds, citation and/or impoundment at owner&#39;s risk and expense.

3.

ALCHOL &amp; RECREATIONAL DRUGS. Alcoholic beverages and recreational drugs are prohibited on all County grounds, Snohomish County Code 10.06.010.

4.

PETS. Pets are not allowed in any county building or in the Equestrian Park. (Exception ). Pets must otherwise be kept on a leash at all times, must not disturb other users of the RV park. Owners are responsible for cleaning up their animals waste.

5.

The RV area shall be kept clean at all times and upon vacating, space shall be left free of debris and waste.

6.

GRAY WATER is not to be drained on the ground, drain gray water in dump stations only.

7.

GARBAGE shall be placed in receptacles only

8.

NO open fires or flames allowed (BBQs ok)

9.

CURFEW. No visitors after midnight Friday and Saturday, 11pm Sunday through Thursday

10.

QUIET TIME. Unnecessary noise is prohibited. Quiet time is 10pm to 8pm, Sun thru Thurs. Midnight on Fri &amp; Sat

11.

Radio, TV, musical instruments and other noise-producing devices shall be operated or used in such a manner and at such times as not to disturb other persons

12.

It is unlawful to discharge firearms, firecrackers, rockets or any other fireworks on County property.

13.

Joyriding of motorbikes, motorcycles, or other sport motor vehicles is prohibited in parking lots or anywhere on the Fairgrounds

14.

Bicycles, skateboards, scooters, roller skates and roller blades are not allowed on the Fairgrounds

15.

It is unlawful to destroy, deface, remove, or disturb any public building, sign, equipment, marker or other structure or property or any natural feature of the Fairgrounds

16.

It is unlawful to incite or participate in riots, or indulge in boisterous, abusive, threatening, or indecent conduct

17.

Household or commercial garbage may not be brought from private property to be dumped

18.
