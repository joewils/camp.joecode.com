Kit Price is a beautiful campground overlooking the North Fork Coeur d&#39;Alene River. Campers enjoy the benefits of the river by fishing along the banks, swimming and floating. Hiking is also a great way to see mountain views.

drivingDirection:

Off I-90, exit at Kingston and go approximately 32 miles north to the campground.

facilitiesDescription:

Amenities include picnic tables, grills, paved parking spurs, accessible vault toilets and drinking water. Kit Price does not have electric hookups.

The campground is situated along the North Fork Coeur d&#39;Alene River at an elevation of 2,550 feet. Campsites are in a wooded area of mixed pine in the river valley of the Cascades. Popular Coeur d&#39;Alene Lake is about 70 miles west of Kit Price.

Wildlife in the region includes mule deer, moose, osprey and calliope hummingbirds.

recreationDescription:

Tubing, swimming and kayaking on the North Fork Coeur d&#39;Alene River are popular summertime activities. Rafters like to head downstream for the class I-III rapids. Catch and release fishing for cutthroat trout is also popular.
