The park is 2 miles from the ocean and is not within walking distance.

The sand dunes are Honeyman&#39;s main attraction, giving campers plenty of hiking opportunities.

There are also 2 fresh water lakes providing fishing, swimming, boating, water skiing, and windsurfing.

The lodge store is open from Memorial Weekend through Labor Day, offering souvenirs, pedal boats, canoes and kayaks to rent.

In late April to the middle of May, the Rhododendrons are in full bloom making it a profusion of color.
