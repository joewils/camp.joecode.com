Tambark Creek Park is located southeast of the City of Mill Creek and offers a mix of recreation opportunities.

The park is blend of natural areas and build environments, providing nature trails, wetland viewing, boardwalks, an artificial turf ballfield, playground, shelters and an off-leash park.
