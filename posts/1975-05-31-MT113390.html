Devil Creek Campground is located 32 miles southwest of West Glacier, Montana, and six miles east of Marias Pass, a high mountain pass located in Glacier National Park.

drivingDirection:

From Hungry Horse head east on US Hwy 2 approximately 45 miles.

From East Glacier Park, take US Hwy 2 west for approximately 19 miles to campground.

facilitiesDescription:

The campground offers 14 overnight sights for tent and RV camping.

Each campsite is equipped with a table and campfire ring with grill.

Accessible vault toilets and drinking water are provided.

Campground roads and parking spurs are paved.
